My Internship Experience


My Internship Journey

I completed my industrial training at Hospital Sultan Ismail Petra as an IT staff intern. During this internship, I gained hands-on experience in technical support, networking, and asset management.

My daily tasks included assisting hospital staff with internet connectivity issues, performing connectivity tests in the server room, checking IT assets, setting up printers, and removing damaged batteries. I also participated in monthly gatherings held at the Rafflesia Auditorium.
